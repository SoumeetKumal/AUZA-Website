Our Commitment

Accessibility Statement

At AUZA, we believe that the web should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to ensuring that our digital presence is inclusive and provides an exceptional experience for all users, regardless of ability.

1. Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with a wide array of disabilities.

2. Measures Taken

To ensure high standards of accessibility, we implement the following measures:

  • Semantic HTML: We use proper HTML structure to ensure compatibility with assistive technologies.
  • ARIA Labels: We provide descriptive labels for interactive elements and non-text content.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Our website is fully navigable via keyboard only.
  • Color Contrast: we maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for all text.
  • Motion Preferences: We respect system-level reduced motion settings to minimize distractions for sensitive users.

3. Browser & Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with modern web browsers and assistive technologies, including screen readers and voice recognition software.

4. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing journey. We regularly audit our website and projects to identify and fix potential barriers. We are constantly learning and evolving our practices to stay at the forefront of inclusive design.
