Our Ongoing Commitment

Accessibility Statement

At AUZA, we believe that digital inclusion is a fundamental right. We are proactively working to ensure that our website and the projects we deliver are accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability.

1. Our Standards

We target the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as our primary benchmark. These standards ensure that web content is Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, and Robust (POUR) for all users.

2. Specific Measures

We don't just follow rules; we design for experience. Our measures include:

  • Native Semantics: Prioritizing native HTML elements over custom ARIA for maximum screen reader reliability.
  • Focus Management: Ensuring a clear, logical focus order for keyboard users.
  • Dynamic Contrast: Maintaining high-contrast ratios well beyond the 4.5:1 minimums.
  • Predictive Navigation: Ensuring consistent navigation patterns throughout the site.

3. Support & Assistance

If you are experiencing difficulty accessing any part of this website, we want to help. Our team is available to assist you in navigating the site or providing information in alternative formats.

  • Support Hours: Monday – Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (GMT+4)
  • Direct Email: hello@auza.mu

4. Reporting Barriers

We aim to be perfect, but the web is dynamic. If you encounter an accessibility barrier on our site, please let us know. Your feedback helps us improve more quickly.

When reporting a barrier, please include:

  • The specific URL or page where you found the issue.
  • The assistive technology you were using (if any).
  • A brief description of what you were trying to do and the barrier encountered.

5. Constant Evolution

Accessibility is a journey of continuous improvement. We regularly perform manual audits and participate in ongoing training to ensure we stay at the cutting edge of inclusive design and development practices.
