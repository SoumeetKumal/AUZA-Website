HOW WE WORK

The "Functional Design" Methodology

We don't just make things look good. We build digital assets that perform.

1. Inclusion as Standard

Most agencies charge extra for accessibility. We don't. Every line of code we write adheres to WCAG 2.1 AA standards because we believe the web should be open to everyone. This also protects you from legal risk and boosts your SEO.

2. Performance is UX

A slow site is a broken site. We optimize images, minify code, and use modern formats like WebP to ensure your site loads in under 2 seconds. This improves conversion rates and Google rankings.

3. Strategy Before Pixels

We never open design software without first understanding your business goals. Who is your audience? What specific action do you want them to take? Your design will be a direct answer to these questions.

4. Component-Based Build

We build scalable design systems, not just static pages. This means your website can grow with your business. Adding new pages or features later becomes cheaper and faster because the foundation is solid.
