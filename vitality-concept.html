01 Overview

The Challenge

Healthcare websites are often cluttered, confusing, and inaccessible. Vitality Health needed a digital presence that reflected their patient-first philosophy—simple, empathetic, and accessible to all.

The main goal was to reduce administrative burden by streamlining the appointment booking process while ensuring full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for patients with disabilities.

Key Challenges

  • Creating a calming, anxiety-reducing digital environment
  • Ensuring complex medical forms were fully accessible
  • Seamless integration with third-party EHR systems
  • Building trust through transparent provider profiles
02 Approach

Our Approach

Discovery Phase

We analyzed patient journeys and found that 60% of users abandoned booking due to complex forms. Our strategy prioritized "Cognitive Ease"—removing friction and using calming visual cues.

Key Insights:

  • Accessibility is Vital: Many patients rely on screen readers or keyboard navigation.
  • Speed Matters: Urgent care seekers need information immediately.
  • Empathy in Design: Soft colors and human imagery build trust faster than clinical white.

Strategic Direction

We adopted a "Health First" visual strategy, using a calming teal palette and rounded, organic shapes to reduce visual stress and convey care.
