01 Overview

The Challenge

Litigation-heavy law firms often have outdated digital presences that fail to convey modern authority. Vanguard needed a site that balanced traditional trust with high-stakes modern litigation excellence.

The primary hurdle was to differentiate the firm in a crowded legal market while ensuring full accessibility compliance for potential clients with diverse needs.

Key Challenges

  • Low client inquiry conversion from mobile devices
  • Weak trust signals for high-stakes corporate clients
  • Accessibility barriers in current digital documents
  • Outdated visual identity that lacked modern litigation edge
02 Approach

Our Approach

Discovery Phase

We conducted an accessibility audit and competitor analysis, finding that most legal sites were 'static billboards' rather than 'functional assets'. Our strategy shifted toward 'Digital Advocacy'.

Key Insights:

  • Trust through Transparency: Showcasing victories clearly and ethically impacts conversion.
  • Accessibility as Inclusivity: Legal services must be accessible to everyone, regardless of disability.
  • Professional Authority: High-end typography and motion can elevate brand perception.

Strategic Direction

The 'Vanguard Standard' focuses on unrelenting preparation and preparation met with aggression. We translated this into a bold, high-contrast digital experience.

Design Language

We utilized a high-contrast visual system to command respect. Large, confident headlines represent the firm's presence in the courtroom, while generous white space reflects their clarity of thought.

Contrast Standard

19:1
WCAG AAA
Compliance

Typography Palette

Playfair for Tradition

Inter for Accessibility

Design Decisions

  • Authority Typography: Using 'Playfair Display' for headlines to evoke tradition, paired with 'Inter' for modern readability.
  • Victory Showcases: Modular sections designed specifically to highlight high-value wins with impact and context.
  • Accessibility-First UI: Guaranteed 4.5:1 contrast ratios and full keyboard navigation for every interactive element.
03 Solution

The Solution

A trust-focused platform with integrated appointment scheduling, WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, and a dedicated 'Recent Victories' showcase. The design uses premium typography and high-contrast visuals to command attention.

Recent Victories Showcase

Dynamic database of firm successes, categorized by practice area for client relevance.

Streamlined Consultation

One-click intake forms optimized for high conversion and screen reader accessibility.

Attorney Portfolios

Detailed profiles highlighting expertise, federal experience, and crisis management skills.

News & Insights Hub

Centrally managed press archive and thought leadership on legal regulation.

Technical Stack

  • Vite + React — Ultra-fast, single-page application experience
  • Tailwind CSS — Precision styling and rapid responsive development
  • Sanity CMS — Flexible content management for law firm updates
  • WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance — Legal-grade digital accessibility
