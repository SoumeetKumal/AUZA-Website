01 Overview

The Challenge

Corporate organizations often struggle with fragmented communication and inaccessible leadership vision. Stratosphere needed a platform that navigated, elevated, and dominated their market space through streamlined digital governance.

The primary goal was to bridge the gap between high-level strategic planning and front-line execution, providing a unified digital hub for stakeholders globally.

Key Challenges

  • Fragmented international communication channels
  • Inconsistent brand application across digital touchpoints
  • Lack of secure, centralized client reporting portals
  • Difficulty in visualizing complex data for rapid decision-making

02 Approach

Our Approach

Discovery Phase

We immersion ourselves in Stratosphere's operational model, conducting stakeholder interviews to identify key friction points. We discovered that existing systems were seen as hurdles rather than facilitators.

Key Insights:
  • User Adoption: Success relied on making tools intuitive for non-technical leadership.
  • Security Concerns: High-stakes consulting requires top-tier data protection protocols.
  • Speed to Insight: Real-time visualization of M&A and digital transformation progress was critical.

Strategic Direction

Our strategy focused on 'The Algorithmic Boardroom' concept—integrating predictive data with clean, authoritative design to empower decisive action.

Design Direction

We established a design system that balances institutional trust with modern performance metrics. Our goal was to create a visual language that feels as rigorous as the strategies themselves.

Core Palette
Typography

Space Grotesk for Authority

Inter for Utility & Readability

Design Decisions

  • Executive Navigation: A 'drill-down' navigation style that allows users to move from global oversight to granular data in three clicks.
  • Data Visualization: Custom, high-contrast charts that emphasize trends over abstract numbers.
  • Professional Palette: A foundation of deep blacks and crisp whites, accented with Stratosphere Blue to convey stability and foresight.

03 Solution

The Solution

We delivered a bespoke corporate platform featuring secure client access, real-time data visualization, and an extensive resource library. The solution prioritizes performance and security while maintaining a premium aesthetic.

Secure Client Portals

Robust, encrypted gatekeeping for sensitive project data and stakeholder reporting.

Real-time Visualization

Dynamic dashboards that track M&A progress and organizational health metrics.

Insight Libraries

Centrally managed repository for thought leadership and proprietary research.

Global CMS

Future-proof content management allowing regional offices to manage localized data safely.

Technical Stack

  • Webflow Enterprise — Secure, scalable content management
  • React Global Dashboards — High-performance data visualization
  • Auth0 Integration — Enterprise-grade authentication
  • WCAG 2.1 AA — Fully accessible digital experience
