Accessibility

Accessibility isn't a checkbox; it's our foundation. We help ambitious businesses expand their reach and improve user experiences for everyone through expert accessibility audits, remediation, and training.

Why Accessibility Matters

Inclusive design isn't just ethical—it's good business. By ensuring your digital products are accessible, you tap into a larger market and provide a better experience for all users, regardless of their situational context or ability.

Our Accessibility Services

  • Accessibility Audits: Comprehensive reviews of your website against WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
  • Remediation: Implementing specific, technical fixes to ensure compliance and improve usability.
  • Inclusive Design Training: Empowering your team to create accessible content and designs from the start.
  • Ongoing Monitoring: Ensuring your website stays accessible as you grow and add new features.

Beyond Compliance

We go beyond meeting legal requirements. We advocate for a "universal design" approach where usability and accessibility are intertwined, leading to better conversion rates and customer satisfaction.
